Arrow-in-i Concepts

The upward arrow replaces the dot on the 'i' in "enviar" — no separate icon needed

1. Hairline Arrow

Thin gold chevron replaces the i-dot. Clean separation between arrow and stem. Minimal, elegant accent.

Digital Banking

2. Monochrome Bold

Arrow in same weight and color as the wordmark. Reads as part of the letterform — seamless integration.

Digital Banking

3. Filled Triangle

Solid gold triangle pointing up. Acts as both the i-dot and a bold brand mark. Impossible to miss.

Digital Banking

4. Connected Arrow

The i-stem extends all the way up through the chevron — one continuous vertical stroke. The letter IS the arrow.

Digital Banking

5. Subtle Lift

Small, tight chevron — nearly the same size as a regular i-dot. You notice it's an arrow on second glance. Understated.

Digital Banking

6. Full Naked Arrow

The original naked arrow placed exactly where 'i' sits. Tallest variant — the arrow extends above the x-height dramatically.

Digital Banking

7. Gold Accent (Light + Dark)

Gold chevron arrow on dark text — works beautifully in both modes. The accent color draws the eye naturally.

8. Wide Wings

Wider chevron arms extending outward. Sheltering, protective feel. Confident and grounded.

Digital Banking

9. Mini Arrow

A complete tiny arrow (shaft + head) floating where the dot would be. Like a micro icon. Playful precision.

Digital Banking
