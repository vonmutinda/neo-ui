Design System v2
Enviar Business
Stripped to essentials. Every surface, every number, every pixel of whitespace is intentional. The interface disappears. The money speaks.
01
Subtract
If you can remove it and nothing breaks, remove it. Borders become shadows. Labels become context. Chrome becomes content.
02
Breathe
Generous whitespace isn't empty space. It's confidence. A financial dashboard that breathes says: we're in control.
03
Honest
Green means money in. Red means money out. Orange means action needed. No other colors. Numbers are always monospace.
Palette
Almost monochrome
Color is rationed. It appears only to communicate status or demand attention. Everything else is grayscale.
